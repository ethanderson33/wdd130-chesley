Shrimp Fried Rice

Our Shrimp Fried Rice is wok-fried to perfection with tender shrimp, fluffy jasmine rice, crisp carrots, sweet peas, fresh green onions, savory soy sauce, garlic, and a touch of sesame oil, creating a rich, aromatic dish that’s both comforting and full of authentic flavor. Allergens: shellfish (shrimp), eggs, soy, sesame, gluten (from soy sauce).

Chow Mein

A generous serving of wok-tossed wheat noodles combined with tender strips of chicken breast, shredded cabbage, carrots, bean sprouts, and onions, all coated in a savory soy-based sauce with garlic, ginger, and a touch of sesame oil for aroma. Finished with fresh scallions for a clean, crisp bite. Allergens: wheat/gluten (noodles, sauce), soy, sesame.

Sweet and Sour Pork

Crispy, golden-fried pork bites tossed in a vibrant sweet and tangy sauce made with pineapple, vinegar, garlic, and a touch of ginger. Served with sautéed bell peppers, onions, and juicy pineapple chunks for a bright, well-balanced flavor. Allergens: wheat/gluten (batter and sauce), soy (in some variations), and trace sesame.

Hot and Sour Soup

A bold, aromatic broth filled with tender tofu, shredded pork, wood ear mushrooms, bamboo shoots, and egg ribbons, balanced with vinegar for tang and white pepper for heat. Finished with scallions and a touch of soy sauce for depth, this classic soup delivers a savory, spicy, and slightly sour profile with every sip. Allergens: soy (tofu, soy sauce), eggs, wheat/gluten (soy sauce), and trace sesame.

Egg Drop Soup

A light, soothing chicken broth swirled with delicate ribbons of beaten egg, creating a silky texture throughout. Seasoned with a touch of white pepper, salt, and a hint of sesame oil, then finished with fresh scallions for brightness. Simple, warm, and comforting—perfect as a starter or side. Allergens: eggs, soy (if soy sauce is used), trace sesame.

Wonton Soup

A comforting, clear chicken broth served with delicate pork wontons filled with ground pork, garlic, ginger, scallions, and soy sauce. Lightly seasoned with white pepper and finished with sliced bok choy or napa cabbage for freshness. Warm, savory, and classic. Allergens: wheat/gluten (wonton wrappers, soy sauce), soy, eggs (in some wrappers), trace sesame.

Potstickers

Pan-seared dumplings with a crisp golden bottom and tender steamed top, filled with a savory blend of ground pork, cabbage, garlic, ginger, scallions, and soy sauce. Served with a light dipping sauce made from soy, rice vinegar, and a touch of chili. Hearty, juicy, and packed with classic dumpling flavor. Allergens: wheat/gluten (dumpling wrappers, soy sauce), soy, eggs (in some wrappers), trace sesame.

Orange Chicken

Crispy, battered chicken pieces tossed in a bright, citrusy glaze made with orange juice, orange zest, soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and a touch of chili for warmth. The sauce balances sweet, tangy, and savory notes, coating every bite with a glossy finish. Served with fresh scallions or orange slices for aroma. Allergens: wheat/gluten (batter and sauce), soy, eggs (in batter), trace sesame.

General Tao's Chicken

Crispy, deep-fried chicken pieces tossed in a bold sweet-and-spicy glaze made with soy sauce, garlic, ginger, chili, and a touch of vinegar for balance. Stir-fried with dried chiles to add heat and finished with scallions for a bright, fresh contrast. A rich, flavorful classic with a perfect crunch. Allergens: wheat/gluten (batter and sauce), soy, eggs (in batter), trace sesame.

Egg Roll

A crunchy, golden-fried roll filled with a savory mix of shredded cabbage, carrots, celery, onions, and seasoned pork, all wrapped in a crisp wheat wrapper. Lightly seasoned with garlic, white pepper, and soy sauce for a classic, satisfying flavor. Served hot with sweet-and-sour or hot mustard dipping sauce. Allergens: wheat/gluten (wrapper, sauce), soy, trace sesame.

Cream Cheese Rangoon

Crispy wonton wrappers filled with a smooth blend of cream cheese, scallions, garlic, and a touch of sugar for a lightly sweet, savory flavor. Fried to a golden crisp and served with sweet-and-sour or plum dipping sauce. A rich, creamy appetizer with a perfect crunch. Allergens: dairy (cream cheese), wheat/gluten (wonton wrapper, sauce), soy (in some sauces), trace sesame.
