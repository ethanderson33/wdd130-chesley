1978 - The Dream Begins

A small family kitchen becomes the birthplace of cherished recipes passed down for generations.

1989 - First Location Opens

The family opens a humble 12-table restaurant, serving homemade noodles, stir-fries, and traditional dumplings.

1994 - Signature Dishes Introduced

Fan favorites like General Tso's Chicken, Spring Rolls, and Cream Cheese Rangoon become permanent staples

1999 - Expansion to a Larger Dining Room

Growing demand leads to a full renovation, doubling seating capacity and updating the dining space.

2004 - Modern Wok Kitchen Installed

New wok ranges and steamers are added, speeding service and improving consistency.

2010 - Family's Second Generation Takes Over

The founders' children refresh the menu, adding lighter options and traditional regional specials.

2015 - Online Ordering Launches

The restaurant join the digital age, offering pickup and delivery through its own website.

2020 - Community Recognition

Awarded "Best Local Chinese Cuisine" and praised for quality, flavor, and hospitality.

Present Day - A Beloved Local Favorite

Still family-owned, still cooking with passion, and still serving the same irresistible dishes that made it a neighborhood staple.
